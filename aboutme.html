👋 Welcome to Food Mood

By Ajitha – Street Food Lover & Entrepreneur

Hi, I'm Ajitha — a passionate food lover and the heart behind this flavorful journey. What started as a simple dream has now become a sizzling street food destination where taste meets tradition.

Food Mood isn’t just a stall. It’s a slice of home served hot and fresh. Inspired by vibrant local flavors and timeless family recipes, every dish here is made with love, soul, and spice — just the way food should be.

From our crispy Veg Burgers to the fiery Masala Dosa and golden crunchy fries, each item is crafted to spark joy and awaken your taste buds. These aren't just meals — they're memories served on a plate.

❤️ What We Believe:

  • Quality First – We use only fresh, hand-picked ingredients.
  • Spotless & Safe – Cleanliness is non-negotiable.
  • You Matter – Every customer leaves with a smile.

At Food Mood, we’re not just feeding hunger — we're fueling happiness. Your support means the world to me, and I’m beyond grateful to have you here.
